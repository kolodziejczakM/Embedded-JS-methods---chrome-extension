This extension download JS methods from developer.mozilla.org and embed one of them on Google website, above the search panel. Author believes that is the very natural way to learn javascript because everyone use google search engine, everyday.
Go to google.com to see how it works.

List of supported Google domains:

.pl - Poland

.co.uk - United Kingdom

.com.ua - Ukraine

.sk - Slovakia

.ru - Russia

.se - Sweden

.ro - Romania

.rs - Serbia

.pt - Portugal

.nl - NetherLands

.lt - Lithuania

.co.jp - Japan

.it - Italy

.hu - Hungary

.es - Spain

.fi - Finland

.cz - Czech Republic

.de - Germany

.ca - Canada

.br - Brazil
